🐟 Tin Pact

Terms, Conditions & Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

About This App

Tin Pact is a private, invite-only web application used by a small group of friends to track their collective tinned fish consumption. It is not a public service and is not available to the general public.

SMS Notifications — Consent & Opt-In

Tin Pact sends SMS notifications to members of the group. Consent to receive SMS messages is obtained directly and explicitly from each recipient by the app owner before any messages are sent. Phone numbers are collected in person or via direct communication, and no messages are sent without prior explicit consent.

By providing your phone number and consenting to receive messages, you agree to receive SMS notifications from Tin Pact including:

  • Notifications when a group member logs a new tin
  • Periodic reminders or group updates related to the tinned fish tracking challenge

Message frequency: Varies based on group activity, typically 1–5 messages per day during the active challenge period.

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your carrier for details.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any message at any time. You will be immediately removed from all future notifications.

To get help: Reply HELP to any message for assistance.

Mobile phone numbers collected for SMS notifications are never sold, shared, or distributed to any third parties for any purpose.

Data We Collect

Tin Pact collects the following information submitted by users:

  • Name (as entered by the user)
  • Number of tins eaten and date
  • Type of tinned fish
  • Optional notes and photos
  • Comments and votes on entries
  • Phone numbers (provided directly to the app owner for SMS notifications only)

Privacy Policy

All data collected is used solely to operate the Tin Pact app for the private group. We do not sell, share, or distribute any personal information to third parties for marketing or any other purpose.

Mobile phone numbers will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Phone numbers are used exclusively to send group notifications via SMS and are never sold, traded, or otherwise disclosed.

Data Storage

App data is stored securely using Supabase. Photos are stored using Cloudinary. SMS notifications are sent via Twilio. These are reputable third-party services with their own privacy and security standards.

Contact

For any questions or to be removed from the notification list, contact the app owner directly.
